# Projects

## Home Garden Planner

Status: Active  
Category: Personal system  
Current stage: Build  
Last reviewed: 2026-05-16

### Purpose

Create a simple seasonal plan for improving a home garden without making the work overwhelming.

### Desired outcome

A practical garden plan that helps the user decide what to plant, what to maintain, and what to leave wild.

### Belongs here

* Planting ideas
* Garden layout notes
* Seasonal maintenance tasks
* Compost, soil, and mulch planning
* Wildlife-friendly garden ideas

### Does not belong here

* Full home renovation tasks
* General shopping lists
* Unrelated outdoor equipment research

### Current next action

Create a short list of the next three garden jobs for this month.

### Related files or artefacts

* local/garden-notes.md
* examples/sample-garden-plan.md

### Privacy notes

May contain location-specific notes. Apply the selected privacy mode before cloud processing.

### Notes

Keep this simple. The goal is to reduce decision fatigue, not create a complicated garden system.

## Family Trip Notes

Status: Exploring  
Category: Family or home  
Current stage: Think  
Last reviewed: 2026-05-16

### Purpose

Collect possible ideas for a family trip before turning them into a confirmed itinerary.

### Desired outcome

A clear shortlist of realistic trip options based on budget, travel time, food needs, and shared interests.

### Belongs here

* Destination ideas
* Travel constraints
* Budget notes
* Food requirements
* Activity preferences
* Itinerary drafts

### Does not belong here

* Bookings that should live in email or calendar
* Passport or payment details
* Sensitive family information

### Current next action

Choose whether this is still an idea collection or ready to become a proper trip plan.

### Related files or artefacts

* local/travel-ideas.md

### Privacy notes

May include family preferences and travel timing. Treat as private by default.

### Notes

Use this only for planning. Do not store sensitive booking details.
